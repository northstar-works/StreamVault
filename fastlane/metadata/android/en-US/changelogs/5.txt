StreamVault v3.0.0 (Build 5)

Major rewrite — native ExoPlayer ITV playback

• Replaced WebView/iframe player with native Android ExoPlayer (Media3)
  - Proper HLS stream handling — no more spinning or black screens
  - Works with gohyperspeed.com and all ITV provider URLs
  - Hardware-accelerated video decoding
  - Handles HLS, DASH, RTSP, progressive streams
• Native fullscreen player with overlay controls
  - Tap to show/hide controls
  - Double-tap to play/pause
  - Back button, play/pause, stream title display
  - Auto-hide controls after 4 seconds
• JavaScript bridge: WebView library → native ExoPlayer seamlessly
• File picker fixed for Android (M3U/JSON import works)
• Landscape orientation for video playback
• Keep screen on during playback
