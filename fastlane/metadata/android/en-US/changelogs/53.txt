StreamVault v4.6.5 (Build 53)

Fixes:
- "Stopping recording" toast no longer fires on channel change, app close, or back button — only fires when you explicitly press the record button to stop a recording you started
- TV DPad: KEYCODE_MEDIA_RECORD (dedicated record key on remotes) and key 183 (yellow button on some Fire TV remotes) now toggle recording
- TV mode setting removed from Settings — now auto-detected by platform (tv vs mobile APK)

Live TV Timeshift:
- New Settings section: "Live TV — Timeshift & DVR"
- Toggle to enable/disable timeshift (pause live TV) globally
- Max buffer duration setting: 10 min, 30 min, 1 hour, or unlimited
- Timeshift enabled state passed to player at launch time

DVR remote storage (new):
- Toggle to enable DVR on a remote network device (FTP, FTPS, or Samba/SMB)
- Configure host, port, credentials, and remote folder path
- Test connection button
- When enabled, a DVR tab appears in the ITV section of the Home page
- All recordings and scheduled recordings route to the remote device when configured

App-wide checks:
- DVR tab hidden from Home Tabs sort list (only appears when enabled in settings)
- playStreamExTs() bridge method added for passing timeshift enabled state to native player
- Resume Plex modal: cancel now safely handled
