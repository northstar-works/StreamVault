StreamVault is a universal streaming media player that lets you organize and play all your video sources in one place — live channels, Plex libraries, IP cameras, local files, network shares, and more.

Features:
• ITV — live stream channels with EPG guide, failover, and locality priorities
• PlexStr — browse and play your Plex server libraries with resume support
• iCam — view RTSP, ONVIF, RTMP, and SRT IP camera streams
• Local — play on-device video files
• Network — stream from Samba/SMB and FTP network shares
• Guide — full EPG TV guide with timeline grid and program info
• DVR — schedule recordings and route to a remote network device
• Live TV timeshift — pause and resume live streams
• Fire TV / Android TV — full DPad remote navigation
• Import playlists via M3U, M3U8, or JSON
• Repository browser with region and category filters
• Smart failover with alternative stream detection
• No accounts, no tracking, no ads — your library stays on your device

StreamVault is built for people who want one clean, private app for every type of video stream.
